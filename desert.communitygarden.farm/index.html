Group or Desert Campout

We have a bus that can take 10 people plus supplies and gear to this location. We take very little, and we leave even less (maybe a seed or two). We rely on the desert and let the desert provide.
"...Cannot afford to be confined to a cell, brainwaves swell, turnin' a desert to a well.." - G.U.R.U. (RIP)

There is always work to be done and items to be needed. We accept court ordered community service from a judge, just as well as conscience ordered volunteer labor from the heart. Service groups, scientists, students, activists, and revolutionary thinkers. Donations of any kind are appreciated and we even will accept donation of money, if that is all you have.
